# Kiwi — Research

## Competitors
| Platform | Model | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|----------|-------|-----------|------------|
| Envato Elements | Subscription | Huge library | Cluttered, low quality floor |
| UI8 | One-time | Curated, high quality | Expensive, no subscription |
| Creative Market | One-time + bundles | Wide variety | Inconsistent quality |
| Mobbin | Subscription | Real app screenshots | No editable files |

## Key Insights
- Designers waste time browsing low-quality results before finding usable assets
- No single platform is tool-agnostic + curated + subscription-based
- Fastest workflow: search → preview → download → open in own tool

## User Needs (18–50, designers)
- Fast discovery (search > browse)
- High quality bar — not 10 000 mediocre files
- Works with their tool (Figma / Sketch / XD / PPT)
- Clear licensing — no legal friction
